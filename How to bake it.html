Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1.5 litter of oil
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 sachets of butter
  • 4 sachets of powder milk
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 0.5 cup of baking powder

How to bake our cookies

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Mix the flour with the sugar,salt and butter.
  3. Add the baking powder and mix again with a little bit of water.
  4. Stand the dough for 25-30mins.
  5. Cut your dough in the sizes of your choice and insert them into the oven.
  6. Bake for 45mins and stop. Remove the cookies and package if you desire it.
